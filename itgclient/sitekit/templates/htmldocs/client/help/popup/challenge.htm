CHALLENGE PHRASE

The Challenge Phrase is simply a word or collection of letters and numbers that you use when accessing the Digital ID Center. The Challenge Phrase is not connected to your Digital ID in any way. You can think of it as a password that lets you access the Web pages that manage your Digital ID. If you forget your Challenge Phrase, you can still use your Digital ID.

You create your Challenge Phrase when you enroll for a Digital ID. You then use the Challenge Phrase when you renew or pick up your Digital ID.
