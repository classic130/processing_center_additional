Finding someone's Digital ID

You can look up someone's Digital ID in an online directory and download the Digital ID to your Web browser. Once you download their Digital ID, you can send them encrypted e-mail. In addition to downloading someone's Digital ID, you can get their ID when they send you a signed e- mail message.

To find someone's Digital ID:

  1. Open Netscape Communicator and choose Communicator > Security Info from the menu.
  2. Click the Certificates link and then click the People link.
  3. Click the Search Directory button.
  4. Select the directory that you want to search from the drop-down menu. If the directory you want is not listed, see Adding a Digital ID directory to Netscape.
  5. Enter the person's e-mail address.
  6. Click the Search button.
  7. Click OK to download the Digital IDs that were found and save them in your address book. (Click Cancel if you do not want to save the Digital IDs that were found.)
