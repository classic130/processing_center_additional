Viewing a Digital ID

When viewing a Digital ID, you see who the ID belongs to, who issued it, what serial number it uses, and so on.

To view your Digital ID:

  1. Start Navigator and choose Options > Security Preferences from the menu.
  2. In the window that appears, click the Personal Certificates tab.
  3. Highlight your Digital ID and click the More Info button. If you have multiple Digital IDs, select the one you want to view and then click More Info.
  4. Your Digital ID information appears in a dialog box. Click OK to close the window.
