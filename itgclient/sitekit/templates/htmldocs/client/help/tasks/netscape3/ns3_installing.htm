Installing a Digital ID

After you enroll for a Digital ID, you receive an e-mail message from the Digital ID Center that contains a PIN and a URL where you can pick up your Digital ID.

To install the Digital ID:

  1. Copy the Digital ID PIN to the clipboard by either selecting the PIN text and choosing Edit > Copy from the menu or pressing Ctrl+C. Click the URL provided in the same e-mail message to return to the Digital ID Center.
  2. Paste your Digital ID PIN into the field on the form. Click the Submit button and follow instructions to complete your Digital ID installation and backup.
  3. Netscape Navigator automatically starts the installation process. Follow the onscreen directions. It is a good idea back up your new Digital ID to a floppy disk and store it in a safe place.
The Congratulations page lets you know you've completed installation successfully.
