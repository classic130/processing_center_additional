Managing the Digital IDs in Netscape

The Netscape browser automatically stores Digital IDs from anyone who sends you a signed message. You can view the Digital IDs, verify the information in the ID, or remove the ID from your computer.

To view someone's Digital ID:

  1. Open Netscape and choose Communicator > Tools > Security Info from the menu.
  2. Click the Certificates link and then click the People link.
  3. Select the person whose Digital ID you want to view.
  4. Click the View/Edit button.
  5. When done viewing the Digital ID, click OK.
You see a dialog box with information about the Digital ID. Click OK to close the dialog box.

To verify someone's Digital ID:

  1. Open Netscape and choose Communicator > Tools > Security Info from the menu.
  2. Click the Certificates link and then click the People link.
  3. Select the person whose Digital ID you want to verify.
  4. Click the Verify button. Communicator checks to see if the Digital ID was issued by a recognized certificate authority and checks the expiration date of the Digital ID.
You see a dialog box verifying the Digital ID. Click OK to close the dialog box.

To delete someone's Digital ID from your computer:

  1. Open Netscape and choose Communicator > Tools > Security Info from the menu.
  2. Click the Certificates link and then click the People link.
  3. Select the person whose Digital ID you want to remove.
  4. Click the Delete button. You'll get a message asking you to confirm the deletion. Click OK.
Once you delete the Digital ID, you cannot send encrypted e-mail to that person. If the person sends you another signed e-mail, their Digital ID will reappear in this list.
