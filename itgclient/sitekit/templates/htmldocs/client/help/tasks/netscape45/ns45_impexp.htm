Importing and Exporting a Digital ID

If you want to use your Digital ID in another application, you can export it from Firefox. And if you have another Digital ID that you want to use in Firefox, you can import the ID.

To import a Digital ID:

  1. Launch the Firefox browser.
  2. From the menu bar, click Tools and then click Options.
  3. Click the Advanced tab and then click the Certificates tab under it.
  4. Click View Certificates and then click the Your Certificates tab.
  5. Click Import, select the Digital ID file to import, and then click Open. Digital ID.
Firefox automatically adds your Digital ID to its certificate database. (Reminder: some applications call Digital IDs certificates.)

To export a Digital ID:

  1. Launch the Firefox browser.
  2. From the menu bar, click Tools and then click Options.
  3. Click the Advanced tab and then click the Certificates tab under it.
  4. Click View Certificates and then click the Your Certificates tab.
  5. Select a Digital ID from the list and then click Backup.
    Alternatively, if you want to export all the Digital IDs (certificates), click Backup All.
  6. Select the directory where you want to export the file, enter a name for the exported file, and then click Save.
  7. If you have set a Master Password for Firefox, you are prompted to enter the password and click OK. If you have not set a Master Password in Firefox, proceed to the next step.
  8. Type a password to protect your Digital ID export file. Retype the password to confirm it. Remember this password because you must enter it when importing your Digital ID into other applications. Your Digital ID is saved with a ".p12" file extension. It is recommended that you save your Digital ID on disk and store the disk in a safety deposit box.
