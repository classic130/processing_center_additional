Setting Digital ID preferences

You can set preferences on Digital IDs to help you manage them. For example, you can mark the Digital ID you prefer others use when sending you encrypted e-mail, and you can remove or display a Digital ID in the online directory.

To set preferences for your Digital ID:

  1. Go to the Digital ID Center and select the Set Preferences form by clicking the Set Preferences button at the top of the page.
  2. Enter the e-mail address, name, or serial number of the Digital ID you want to set. You do not need to enter all three pieces of information. Searching by e-mail address is the easiest method, because you are most likely to know the exact e-mail address registered with your ID.
    The information you enter must exactly match the information in the Digital ID. For example, if you look up a Digital ID by specifying the name Marj Baldwin, only the Digital ID that contains the name Marj Baldwin would be found. Digital IDs under the names Marjorie Baldwin or M. Baldwin would not be returned as matches.
  3. Select the type of Digital ID you want to replace. If you are not sure whether the Digital ID you are looking for is valid, revoked, expired, or pending, select "All" for a comprehensive search, and then click the Search button.
  4. You see a list of Digital IDs to choose from. Click the link for the Digital ID that you want to set.
  5. On the Web page that appears, configure the preferences you want. The preferences are as follows:
    Mark this Digital ID as my preferred S/MIME certificate This indicates that you want people to use this Digital ID when sending you encrypted e-mail. To avoid confusion, use this preference for one Digital ID only.
    Display my Digital ID in the online directory of Digital IDs This option makes it easy for others to find your Digital ID. People need your Digital ID before they can send you encrypted e-mail. Note that you can also send them a signed e-mail message, which is another way of giving them your Digital ID.
    Enter your Challenge Phrase in the field at the bottom of the form, and then click the Submit button.
