Viewing a Digital ID

You can view your own Digital IDs or you can view information in Digital IDs that belong to other people who have sent you their Digital ID.

To view your own Digital IDs:

  1. Start Internet Explorer and choose View > Internet Options from the menu.
  2. Click the Content tab and then click the Personal button.
  3. Select the Digital ID you want to view and then click View Certificate.
  4. The information appears in a dialog box. Click OK to close the window.

To view another person's Digital ID:
  1. Start Internet Explorer and choose View > Internet Options from the menu.
  2. Click the Content tab and then click the Edit Profile button.
  3. Select the Digital ID tab.
  4. Select the e-mail address for the person whose Digital ID you want to view.
  5. Select the ID you want to view from the list. Typically, there is only one ID per person, but there might be multiple IDs.
  6. Click Properties. The information appears in a dialog box. Click OK to close the window.
