Exchange Enrollment form

This page describes the fields on the enrollment form in the Digital ID Center. You use your Web browser to go to the Digital ID Center, and then click the Enroll button to view this screen.

Exchange sends information to the Digital ID Center. The enrollment form displays this information. Confirm the information and then click the Submit button. The information on the form is listed below.

Full Name is your name. When someone looks up your name in the directory, the Digital ID Center uses the information you supply here. Therefore, use a name people are familiar with and likely to use when searching for you.

E-mail Address is your complete e-mail address, including company/domain name; for example, jdoe@symantec.com. Your e-mail address is included in your Digital ID so it can be used with e-mail applications to digitally sign and encrypt or decrypt e-mail messages. Make sure your e-mail address is correct! Without the correct address, you will not receive the PIN (Personal Identification Number), which is necessary to receive your Digital ID.

Challenge Phrase
The Challenge Phrase is a password you use when you want to manage your Digital ID throughout the Digital ID Center. It is not stored with the Digital ID. Before you can manage your Digital ID, you need to enter the Challenge Phrase. The Challenge Phrase protects you from unauthorized management of your Digital ID. The Challenge Phrase can be one or more words, but it cannot include any punctuation. Do not forget this phrase. If you need to write it down, make sure you store it in a safe place.
