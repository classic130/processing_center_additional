Viewing a Digital ID

You can view your own Digital IDs or you can view information in Digital IDs that belong to other people who have sent you their Digital ID.

To view your own Digital IDs:

  1. Start Internet Explorer and choose Tools > Internet Options from the menu.
  2. Click the Content tab and then click the Certificates button.
  3. Select the Personal tab and then select the Digital ID you want to view. Click the View button at the bottom of the dialog box.
  4. The information appears in a dialog box. Click OK to close the window.

To view another person's Digital ID:
  1. Start Internet Explorer and choose Tools > Internet Options from the menu.
  2. Click the Content tab and then click the Certificates button.
  3. Select the Other People tab.
  4. Select the person whose Digital ID you want to view.
  5. Click View. The information appears in a dialog box. Click OK to close the window.
