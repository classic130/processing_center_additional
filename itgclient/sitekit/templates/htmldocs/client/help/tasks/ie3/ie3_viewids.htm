Viewing a Digital ID

When viewing a Digital ID, you see who the ID belongs to, who issued it, what serial number is uses, and so on. You can view your Digital ID or any other Digital IDs you have (such as those you use when sending secure e-mail to people).

To view your Digital ID:

  1. Start Internet Explorer and choose View > Options from the menu.
  2. In the window that appears, click the Security tab.
  3. Click the Personal button.
  4. Highlight your Digital ID and click the View Certificate button. If you have multiple Digital IDs, select the one you want to view and then click View Certificate.
  5. Your Digital ID information appears in a dialog box. Click OK to close the window.
To view a Digital ID from a CA:
  1. Start Internet Explorer and choose View > Options from the menu.
  2. In the window that appears, click the Security tab.
  3. Click the Sites button.
  4. Highlight the Digital ID you want to view and click the View Certificate button.
  5. The Digital ID information appears in a dialog box. Click OK to close the window.
