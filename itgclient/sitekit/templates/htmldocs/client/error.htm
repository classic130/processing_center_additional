ActiveX Control Download Error
You may be using a Windows XP Service Pack 2 computer.

You may have encountered this error because of the following reasons -

  • You may have chosen 'Cancel' when asked to install VeriSign's ActiveX Control(s)- You must install the ActiveX Controls to proceed. If you choose to install the ActiveX Controls, click here and follow the download instructions. Do not use the back button on your browser. You need to be a Power User or a Local Administrator on this computer and you need to allow ActiveX Controls in your browser. If you are not sure whether you have the correct permissions contact your system administrator.

  • You may have clicked 'No' when prompted to download the ActiveX Control(s)- You must install the ActiveX Controls to proceed. If you choose to install the ActiveX Controls, click here and click Yes when prompted to download the ActiveX Controls. Do not use the back button on your browser.
