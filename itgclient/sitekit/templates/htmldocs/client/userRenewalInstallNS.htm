What Do You Do Next?
Your Digital ID has been delivered to your web browser. To ensure that your ID was properly installed follow the instructions below:

  1. (On a Windows system) Launch the Firefox browser, click Tools and then click Options.
    (On a Mac system) Launch the Firefox browser, click Firefox and then click Preferences.
  2. Click the Advanced tab and then click the Certificates tab.
  3. Click View Certificates and then click the Your Certificates tab.
    Here you can view all your certificates.
