Digital ID Could Not Be Installed

The most likely reasons for this problem are:
  • You may have already installed the Digital ID earlier.
  • The browser you are using now is not the same one you used when you enrolled for your Digital ID. You must use the same browser, on the same computer, to enroll and install your Digital ID.
  • The certificate database in your Web browser is not accepting your new Digital ID. (The database may be corrupted.) Install the browser again and re-enroll for an ID.
