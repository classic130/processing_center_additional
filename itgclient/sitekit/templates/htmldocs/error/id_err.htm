Unable to verify identity

VeriSign verifies your identity by checking the Digital ID enrollment information you provided against the data in the Equifax consumer database. We were unable to verify your identity because the information you provided does not match the information in the database. This may be due to missing, outdated, or inaccurate information in the database.

One of the most common problems is outdated address information. If you have moved in the past two years, please try enrolling again, and include your previous address.

Another common problem is a mismatch in the address information. For example, if your home address is listed in the database and you listed your business address in your Digital ID enrollment, your information will not match.

If you suspect that your identity verification failed because the Equifax database contains incorrect information, you may want to review a copy of your credit report. For instructions on ordering your credit report from Equifax, or updating your database information, call:
