There was an error while signing your HTML transaction. This was because the HTML that was provided for signing contained one or more invalid tags. The list below enumerates the tags and corresponding error codes.

  • 0x80001102 - The foreground and background colors do not have enough separation or contrast.
  • 0x80001103 - Scripts, JavaScript or VBScript
  • 0x80001104 - EMBEDS
  • 0x80001105 - FORMS
  • 0x80001106 - FRAMES
  • 0x80001107 - IMAGES
  • 0x80001108 - APPLETS
  • 0x80001109 - PLUGINS
  • 0x8000110A - STYLESHEETS
  • 0x8000110B - OTHER TAGS viz. INPUT, LINK, SELECT, OPTION, FRAME, FRAMESET, ILAYER, LAYER, TEXTAREA, SPAN, BGSOUND, MARQUEE, META, A
  • 0x8000110C - The foreground and/or background colors that you have specified do not have enough separation or contrast with the user's default browser settings.

Please check the HTML that you want to sign and remove any instance of the above tags. If the problem persists, please contact your administrator.
